# BetterAt for Johns Hopkins School of Nursing

## Evidence-Based Clinical Competency Tracking

---

### The Challenge

Nursing programs face a growing accountability gap between clinical experience and competency evidence.

- **Clinical competency is a black box.** Students complete shifts, write reflections, and preceptors sign off -- but nobody has real-time visibility into which specific competencies each student has actually demonstrated.

- **AACN Essentials compliance is manual.** Tracking 50+ competencies across 200+ students using spreadsheets and paper portfolios is time-consuming and error-prone.

- **Preceptor feedback is fragmented.** Hospital preceptors supervise your students but lack an easy, structured way to provide competency-level feedback. Paper forms go unfilled.

- **Student preparation is invisible.** Faculty can't distinguish students who plan their clinical learning from those who show up unprepared.

- **NCLEX gaps surface too late.** Competency holes become visible at board exams -- not weeks earlier when intervention is still possible.

---

### The Solution

BetterAt is a clinical competency tracking platform that makes every clinical hour produce structured, assessable evidence.

**For Students:**
- Plan each clinical shift with specific competency targets and sub-steps
- Capture evidence naturally via text message, voice notes, or photos -- no new app required
- AI coaching helps prepare for shifts and reflect on performance
- Self-assess against AACN competencies after each session

**For Preceptors:**
- 2-minute mobile validation: see student evidence, rate competency, add notes
- Card-based interface -- easier than any paper form
- No app download required (works via mobile web)

**For Faculty:**
- Real-time cohort dashboard showing competency achievement across all students
- AACN domain heatmap: 10 domains across all students, color-coded by progress
- Gap alerts: instantly see when a majority of the cohort has zero attempts in a competency area
- NCLEX readiness view: flag at-risk students weeks before boards
- Drill into any student's full competency history with evidence trail

**For the Dean:**
- Real-time AACN Essentials compliance data for accreditation
- Early warning system for NCLEX pass rate protection
- Demonstrable innovation in clinical education

### How It Works

**A typical Tuesday for a BSN student:**

1. **Before shift** -- The student opens BetterAt and plans their clinical focus: "IV medication administration and patient assessment." They link their plan to AACN competencies and create a sub-step checklist.

2. **During shift** -- The student texts their AI coach: "Just did my first solo IV push -- vancomycin 1g over 60 min. Preceptor said I did well on rate calculation but need to improve site assessment." They send a photo of their clinical notes. The AI logs evidence automatically.

3. **After shift** -- The preceptor opens the validation dashboard on their phone. They see the student's evidence and self-assessment. They rate "Medication Safety Practices" as Satisfactory with a note. Done in 90 seconds.

4. **Faculty view** -- The professor opens the cohort dashboard. They see 30 students' competency progress across 10 AACN domains. One student is trending upward in Quality & Safety. Three students are flagged red for gaps in Population Health. Faculty pushes a targeted learning module.

5. **8 weeks before graduation** -- The NCLEX readiness view shows 5 students below 50% validated competencies. Specific gaps are identified. Targeted remediation begins immediately -- not after a failed board exam.

### Competency Framework

BetterAt ships pre-configured with 50 AACN-aligned core competencies organized across all 10 AACN Essentials domains:

1. Knowledge for Nursing Practice
2. Person-Centered Care
3. Population Health
4. Scholarship for the Nursing Discipline
5. Quality and Safety
6. Interprofessional Partnerships
7. Systems-Based Practice
8. Informatics and Healthcare Technologies
9. Professionalism
10. Personal, Professional, and Leadership Development

Each competency tracks through a clear progression:

**Not Started > Learning > Practicing > Checkoff Ready > Validated > Competent**

With a three-actor validation chain:

**Student self-assesses > Preceptor validates > Faculty approves**

### What BetterAt Replaces -- and What It Doesn't

BetterAt is **not** an LMS replacement, an EHR training tool, a scheduling system, or NCLEX prep. It works alongside Canvas, Blackboard, simulation labs, clinical rotation schedules, and test prep platforms like UWorld, Kaplan, and ATI.

**BetterAt is the connective tissue between clinical experience and competency evidence.** It turns "I think my students are ready" into "I can prove my students are ready."

### Frequently Asked Questions

**"We already have a clinical tracking system."**
Does it show you which specific competencies a student has demonstrated evidence for -- or just that they showed up for their shift?

**"Students won't use another app."**
They already text about their shifts. BetterAt works through messaging -- no new app to download. We just make their existing behavior productive.

**"What about HIPAA?"**
Students never photograph patients. Evidence is procedure documentation, self-reflection, and skill demonstration -- the same content as current paper portfolios, just structured and searchable.

**"Our preceptors are too busy."**
That's exactly why we built a 2-minute card interface. It's easier than the paper form they're already ignoring.

**"Do we need IRB approval?"**
This is a quality improvement and educational tool, not research. Same category as switching from paper to digital portfolios.

**"What about LMS integration?"**
We start standalone for the pilot. Once it proves value, we can discuss Canvas or Blackboard grade passback in a subsequent phase.
