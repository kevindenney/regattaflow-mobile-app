How Many Can We Pass?
When you find yourself at the wrong end of the fleet, don’t
get depressed. You are in The Land of Opportunity—there is
a whole fleet of boats waiting to be passed! Don’t wait for a
miracle to save you. Get to work and grind ’em down, one at
a time. You’re not going to win this race; that is no longer the
goal. Actually, winning is redefined for this race: Winning is
passing as many boats as you can.
Sail Fast & Go the Right Way
Don’t panic. Settle down and work on boat speed. You
will not pass anyone without good speed. Concentrate on
speed, and you should be able to knock off a few tail-enders
easily.
