1.4 Performance Analysis
So, how are your skills?
The Performance Analysis presented here is intended to
help you look at your racing skills and the overall skills of the
crew on the boat you race. If you are a tactical king, you need
to team up with a boat speed druid and a boat handling wizard.
Of course, if you race single handed you’ll need to be all these
things!
Consider both strengths and weaknesses. Obviously, you
want to eliminate weaknesses. As we shall see, there are also
ways to shape races toward your strengths.
